🎬 Film Title

The Seventh Guest

✍️ Genre & Tone

Murder Mystery / Psychological Thriller — slow-burn, atmospheric, 1930s English manor.

📝 Logline

When a wealthy aristocrat is found dead at his own dinner party, each of the seven guests holds a secret — and only one of them is a killer.

🩸 The Victim

Lord Harrington — found slumped in the library, a poisoned glass of brandy at his side. The evening had barely begun.

🎭 Setting

Harrington Manor, rural England. October 1934. A storm has cut the telephone lines. No one can leave.

💡 The Central Mystery

Who is the seventh guest — the one who was not invited — and why did they come?

🎥 Production Notes
🎬 Director's Vision

A slow-burn psychological thriller. Every frame a painting — candlelit interiors, shadows that lie, faces that hide everything.

📸 Visual Style

Deep burgundy, ivory, candlelight amber. 2.39:1 aspect ratio. Handheld for tension, locked-off for reveals. Natural light where possible.

🎵 Music & Sound Direction

String quartet. Discordant notes under key scenes. Silence used deliberately. Thunder and rain as atmosphere throughout.

🎽 Costume Direction

1930s formal English dinner attire. Each character's clothing reflects their personality and secrets. Colours chosen deliberately.
